Intro

With the vision to be a "World Class Leader of Engineering Research providing Green Alternative" Green Lantern Co. provides quality product(s) and a competative price. Their first product, Green Torchlight is a torchlight that convert mechanical energy to electrical energy thus removing the need to plug into a power socket. This product made a cleaner impact to our Earth by removing the need to change the battery which is earth mineral.

Green Lantern Co. was established in 2017, located at Kuala Lumpur

Behind the Company, there's 5 student from Kulliyyah of Engineering of IIUM, who strive to save the earth and provide Islamic values in all of their engineering solution.
